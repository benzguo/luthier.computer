## List of subprocessors

_Last updated: June 8, 2020_

Tray uses some third party subprocessors, including cloud computing providers and payment processors, to provide our services.

- [Stripe](https://stripe.com/privacy-center/legal). Payment processing services provider.
- [Amazon Web Services](https://aws.amazon.com/compliance/gdpr-center/). Cloud services provider.
- [Vercel](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy). Cloud services provider.
- [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com/gdpr/introduction/). Cloud services provider.
- [Simple Analytics](https://docs.simpleanalytics.com/what-we-collect). Analytics provider.
